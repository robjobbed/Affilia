# Affilia Business Model & Pricing

## üí∞ Platform Fee Structure

**Affilia takes a 5% cut of every transaction.**

### How It Works

For a **$50,000 contract**:
- **Contract Value**: $50,000
- **Platform Fee (5%)**: $2,500
- **Freelancer Receives**: $47,500

### Who Pays the Platform Fee?

The platform fee is handled differently based on the payment structure:

#### 1. **Upfront Payment** (Freelancer Pays Fee)
- Hirer pays: **$50,000**
- Platform keeps: **$2,500** (5%)
- Freelancer receives: **$47,500** (95%)
- ‚úÖ Best for freelancers (get paid immediately)

#### 2. **Milestone Payments** (Freelancer Pays Fee)
- Hirer pays: **$50,000** (split across milestones)
- Platform keeps: **$2,500** (5%, collected across milestones)
- Freelancer receives: **$47,500** (95%, distributed across milestones)
- ‚úÖ Balanced risk for both parties

#### 3. **Pay After Completion** (Hirer Pays Fee) ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Contract value: **$50,000**
- Freelancer cost: **$50,000** (covered by platform until paid)
- Platform fee: **$2,500** (5%)
- **Hirer pays**: **$52,500** (contract + fee)
- Freelancer receives: **$50,000** (100% of contract value)
- ‚úÖ Best for hirers (pay only after delivery)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Hirer covers the platform's cost of fronting payment

## üìä Payment Structure Options

### Option 1: Pay 100% Upfront
```
Timeline:    Contract Signed ‚Üí Immediate Payment ‚Üí Work Begins ‚Üí Delivery
Hirer pays:  $50,000 (day 1)
Risk:        Hirer assumes all risk
Best for:    Established freelancers, smaller projects
```

### Option 2: Pay in Milestones
```
Example: 50/50 Split
Milestone 1: $25,000 (upfront)
Milestone 2: $25,000 (on completion)

Timeline:    Contract ‚Üí Pay 50% ‚Üí Midpoint Delivery ‚Üí Pay 50% ‚Üí Final Delivery
Hirer pays:  $50,000 total (split across milestones)
Risk:        Shared between both parties
Best for:    Most projects, balanced approach
```

Common milestone structures:
- **2 Milestones**: 50% / 50%
- **3 Milestones**: 33% / 34% / 33%
- **4 Milestones**: 25% / 25% / 25% / 25%
- **Custom**: Flexible percentages (must total 100%)

### Option 3: Pay After Completion
```
Timeline:    Contract Signed ‚Üí Work Begins ‚Üí Delivery ‚Üí Payment
Hirer pays:  $52,500 (after delivery)
            = $50,000 contract + $2,500 platform fee
Risk:        Freelancer assumes all risk
Best for:    Trusted hirers, risk-averse companies
```

**Why the extra 5%?**
When you pay after completion, Affilia fronts the entire payment to the freelancer. You're paying the platform fee to compensate for this financial risk and service.

## üí° Why This Model?

### For Freelancers
- **95% of contract value** when you get paid upfront/milestones
- **100% of contract value** when hirer pays after (they cover the fee)
- **Secure payments** through Apple Pay
- **No payment disputes** - platform handles escrow

### For Hirers
- **Flexible payment options** based on your risk tolerance
- **Pay after delivery** option protects you from non-delivery
- **Transparent pricing** - you always know what you'll pay
- **Milestone control** - release payments as work is completed

### For Platform (Affilia)
- **Sustainable 5% fee** on all transactions
- **Incentivized to keep both parties happy**
- **Revenue scales with marketplace growth**
- **Fair risk distribution** based on payment timing
